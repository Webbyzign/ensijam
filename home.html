PRO SERVICE

Our PRO (Public Relations Officer) Services ensure smooth and compliant interaction with government departments. From visa processing and labor approvals to trade license renewals and document attestation, Ensijam Business LLC handles all your administrative and legal formalities—saving you time, effort, and penalties.

MEDICAL & ID TYPING

Ensijam Business LLC provides fast and accurate Medical and Emirates ID Typing Services for all visa-related processes. We assist with medical fitness application typing, Emirates ID pre-registration, appointment scheduling, and form submissions—ensuring a smooth and error-free experience for both individuals and businesses.

AGREEMENT TYPING

At Ensijam Business LLC, we offer precise and legally formatted Agreement Typing Services tailored to your business needs. From tenancy contracts and partnership agreements to MOUs and employment contracts, our team ensures that all documents meet official standards and are ready for attestation or submission

SPONSORSHIP

Ensijam Business LLC offers reliable local sponsorship solutions for businesses looking to establish a presence in the UAE. We connect you with trustworthy Emirati sponsors and handle all legal arrangements—ensuring full compliance, transparency, and long-term partnership security for your company formation.

TASHEEL SERVICES

Ensijam Business LLC provides comprehensive Tasheel Services to streamline all your Ministry of Human Resources and Emiratisation (MOHRE) transactions. From labor contract typing and quota applications to work permit processing and company registration, our expert team ensures fast, compliant, and hassle-free service for all your labor-related needs.

GOLDEN VISA SERVICE

Ensijam Business LLC offers professional support for obtaining the UAE Golden Visa. We assist investors, professionals, and talents with eligibility checks, documentation, and submission—ensuring a smooth, efficient, and compliant process from start to finish.

A SERVICE ONLINE NOTARY SERVICES

Ensijam Business LLC offers secure and convenient Online Notary Services, allowing you to notarize documents remotely without visiting an office. From power of attorney and affidavits to contracts and legal declarations, we ensure fast, legally valid, and government-approved digital notarization—saving your time while maintaining full compliance.

CERTIFICATE ATTESTATION SERVICES

Ensijam Business LLC offers fast, reliable certificate attestation for educational, personal, and commercial documents—approved by embassies and ministries—for use in the UAE and internationally.
